Who We Are

Community Radio 98.5 FM is a locally owned and operated radio station dedicated to serving our community with quality programming, local news, and a platform for diverse voices.

Founded in 1985 by a group of local journalists and radio enthusiasts, we've grown from a small AM station to the leading FM broadcaster in the region, while maintaining our commitment to community values.

Our team of dedicated professionals works around the clock to bring you the best in music, talk shows, and up-to-the-minute local news coverage.

Our Mission

"To inform, entertain, and connect our community through high-quality broadcasting that reflects the diversity and spirit of our region, while maintaining the highest standards of journalistic integrity and community service."

Our History

1985 - Founding

Community Radio begins broadcasting on AM 1600 with a small team of volunteers and a 1,000-watt transmitter.

1992 - FM Transition

The station moves to 98.5 FM, expanding coverage to the entire county with a 50,000-watt signal.

2005 - Digital Upgrade

Full digital broadcasting equipment installed, allowing for HD Radio and online streaming.

2015 - 30th Anniversary

Celebrated three decades of service with a community festival attended by over 5,000 people.

2023 - Present Day

Now reaching over 250,000 weekly listeners through broadcast and digital platforms.
